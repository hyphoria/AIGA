Directions

Subway TTC

From Metro Hall vicinity, take the northbound Yonge-University-Spadina subway line to Downsview Station. (St. Andrew Station is closest to Metro Hall.). At Downsview, transfer to the 196A ROCKET EXPRESS TO YORK UNIVERSITY bus. Get off at The Pond Rd. and Sentinel Rd. The TEL Building is one block west. (allow about an hour, $3)

From the Airports

From Pearson Airport, taxi service will run $40-50. From Billy Bishop Airport, take the Porter shuttle to Union Station and follow subway directions.
